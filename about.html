About Emtithal

A Saudi company advancing environmental compliance and integrated pest management.

Who We Are

Emtithal operates across environmental consulting and pest control: environmental impact studies, mobile air quality monitoring, waste management & rehabilitation, and standardized pest control for residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

Our strategic goal aligns with Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030—sustainability, resource protection, and continuous quality improvement.

NCEC License: ELESL-2024-000425
Membership: National Pest Management Association
Expertise: 13+ years (Director of IPM) in KSA & UK
Standards: ISO 9001 · ISO 14001 · ISO 45001 (application & systems)

Mission · Vision · Values

Mission

Support and qualify clients to apply robust quality and environmental management systems—and compete effectively in the local market.
